We Hire


As one of my assignments in the Advanced Web Design Studio class. I also created a website to help students and recruiters connect at Career fairs. The main goal of the application was to provide a platform to both parties to effortlessly communicate announcements and/or queries with one another.



The recruiters could see the list of all the students registered for the event and vice versa. They could also view all the messages sent by them on their own profile. I used jQuery and socket.io to provide seamless communication between multiple users.



The site allowed users to reply to specific messages in real-time. It showed the most recent messages at the top to ensure that both students and recruiters are viewing the latest information at all times.
